Is it really free to build?
Yes. AI lets us build a real, custom website at a fraction of the old cost, so this summer the design is free (a basic professional site normally runs ~$2,500). You see it before you pay, and you only ever pay for hosting & care — from $97/mo.
What's the difference between $97 and $197?
Basic ($97/mo) is a clean one-page site with hosting, your domain, business email, security, and 30 days of free changes. Standard ($197/mo) adds the lead-getters — a contact form, plus doubles your free-changes window to 60 days — so your site actively brings you business. (An AI chat widget, online booking, and SEO come as add-ons or in a Custom build.)
What if I want changes after it's live?
Your first 30 days (60 on Standard) include free changes — text edits, photo swaps, and contact-detail updates — dialed in until it's right. After that, small updates are part of your plan, and bigger changes are quick to request. Call us anytime and a real person helps.
What's my web address — do I need a domain?
You go live instantly on a free yourbiz.oneapp.site address — no domain needed. Want your own custom domain (like yourbusiness.com)? You buy and own it at any registrar (so you keep control and the renewal), and we connect and manage it for you. Already have one? We'll send you the exact steps to point it over, or Jennifer walks you through it.
What if I don't buy — can I try again later?
Your free preview is your one free design. If you leave without keeping it, that design is removed. You're welcome to come back for a brand-new design anytime for a small $79 design fee — the AI builds you a fresh one to review.
Can I upgrade later?
Absolutely. Start Basic and move up whenever you want more. SEO, Google Business & reviews, bookings, a mobile app, a full dashboard — we can build all of it on top. Just ask and we'll quote it.
